## Full Description *(used for both stores — max 4,000 characters)*

**Find out if your home is right for plug-in solar panels — in minutes.**

SolarSnap helps homeowners assess whether their home is suitable for plug-in (balcony) solar panels. No ladders, no engineer, no jargon. Just stand at your proposed installation spot, hold your phone toward the sky in the direction the panel would face, and tap Assess.

**How it works**

1. Stand where you plan to mount the panel — on a balcony railing, a south-facing wall, or a garden fence.
2. Hold your phone up toward the sky at the angle and direction the panel would face.
3. Tap Assess. SolarSnap photographs the sky, reads your GPS location and compass bearing, and delivers a verdict in seconds.

**What SolarSnap Basic includes**

- A clear suitability verdict: Excellent, Good, Fair, or Poor
- Sky obstruction analysis — the app detects buildings, trees, and other objects that would shade the panel
- Sun position data showing where the sun is right now relative to your panel
- Assessments must be taken within 200 metres of your registered home location

**What SolarSnap Premium adds**

- Estimated annual energy yield in kWh, based on real EU solar irradiance data from the PVGIS dataset (European Commission Joint Research Centre)
- Estimated annual saving in £, based on current UK electricity prices — adjustable to your own tariff
- A month-by-month energy breakdown showing how output varies across the year
- Adjust the panel capacity to match your specific equipment and recalculate instantly

**No technical knowledge needed**

SolarSnap is designed for homeowners, not engineers. The results are explained in plain language and the assessment takes less than 30 seconds.

**Important notes**

- Yield estimates are based on standard conditions using long-term solar irradiance averages. Actual output will vary depending on local weather, shading, panel efficiency, and inverter losses.
- Assessments require GPS and camera access.
- A personal licence (Basic or Premium) allows assessments within approximately 200 metres of your registered home location. Commercial licences for professional use are available separately.
